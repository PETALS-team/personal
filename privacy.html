Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

This site (the "PETALS Solo Check-in") is a small standalone tool that lets you run the PETALS framework on your own. It is not the main PETALS application, and this policy covers this site only.

No data storage

We don't run a server or database behind this site. Every check-in you complete stays entirely in your browser. When you generate a "compare" link, your scores are encoded directly into that link's web address — nothing is ever sent to us or stored on our systems. Close the tab, and it's gone.

No tracking or analytics

This site does not use cookies, does not run any analytics (not even privacy-friendly or aggregate ones), and does not include any third-party trackers or pixels. We don't know who visits this site or how you use it.

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Like any web host, Cloudflare's network necessarily processes standard connection information (such as IP address and request logs) to serve the page and protect it from abuse. This happens at the infrastructure level, is outside our control, and is not used by us for tracking or analytics. See Cloudflare's privacy documentation for details.

Sharing to third parties

If you choose to share your flower to X (Twitter) or LinkedIn, that action opens those platforms directly and is entirely optional. Any data handling from that point on is governed by their own privacy policies, not ours.

The main PETALS app

This promotional site is separate from the main PETALS product. If you're looking for the privacy policy that covers PETALS accounts and the main application, please visit usepetals.com for full details.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised accordingly.

Contact

For any privacy questions, get in touch via usepetals.com.
